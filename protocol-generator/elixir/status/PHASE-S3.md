## Idiom seams (deliberate, vs C#/TS/OCaml — see A-ELX-006)

The **actor model** — the most distant concurrency seam of the four peers.
`{:ok,_}/{:error,_}` tagged tuples (not exceptions/`result`) · a **GenServer-owned
store** serializing all mutable state (no locks; atomic CAS in one call) · **one
process per connection** as the single writer (subsumes OCaml's write mutex) ·
**selective `receive`** for the §6.11 outbound demux (subsumes the condition
variable) · the `system/protocol/connect` handshake handled inline while every
other EXECUTE is dispatched on a spawned process (§4.8), so a §6.13(b) reentry
await never stalls the reader.
